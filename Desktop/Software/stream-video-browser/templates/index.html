Get Started

To start, follow these 3 easy steps.

Find a clear space to work on.

The Any Instrument app will use the surroundings from your webcam to "build" your instrument. You will want a clear space to move your arms around and play.

Find the instrument for you.

Search through the instruments and decide which you are most interested in learning to play! If you're a beginner and are not sure yet, view our tab to learn more about each instrument and decide which is right for you.

Start playing.

Once you've decided on your instrument and have found the right space, select "play" and you'll start playing your selected instrument.
